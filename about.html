La Martiniere Calcutta Model United Nations 2013

A little bit about us :

A model UN conference is a simulation of the proceedings that take place at the United Nations conferences. It aims to educate participating students about current affairs,topics in international relations,diplomacy and the United Nations agenda.
Students of La Martiniere Schools,Calcutta,India,have been hosting LMCMUN-an annual event ,since 2009. This conference is organised and run entirely by the students under the guidance of faculty advisors on the school premises.

We at La Martiniere take great pride in the fact that our MUN conference was the first of its kind in eastern India, and believe in working towards providing a more comprehensive and exciting conference experience every year.From a small fledgling in 2009 with 30 participating city schools LMCMUN grew and a national level conference was successfully hosted in 2010.After our resounding success in 2012 we are ready to host our fourth LMCMUN in May 2013.

The upcoming conference not only aims at seeing students from all over the country work in an environment representing the United Nations, tackling issues of international concern through deliberation and negotiation thus honing their skills of oration and debate.We will also work to ensure an enjoyable experience for student delegates, giving them a chance to interact with other students and challenge themselves at the same time.
